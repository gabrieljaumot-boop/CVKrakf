Frequently Asked
Questions

How much does YourCVy cost?

Previewing is completely free — try all 288 combinations unlimited times. Downloading costs just €5 one-time. No subscription, no hidden fees, no account needed.

What file formats can I upload?

Both PDF and Word (.docx) are fully supported. Our AI automatically extracts your name, work history, education, skills, and languages from either format.

Is my CV data private?

100% private. Your CV is processed entirely in your browser — your personal data never leaves your device and is never stored on any server. Fully GDPR compliant.

Are the CVs ATS-friendly?

Yes. Every layout uses clean semantic HTML engineered to pass Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) used by 98% of Fortune 500 companies and major recruitment agencies.

Do I need to create an account?

No account, no email, no registration — ever. Upload, preview 288 designs, edit your content, and download entirely anonymously.

How many design combinations are there?

288 unique combinations: 12 professional layouts (sidebar, timeline, creative, executive, academic, tech terminal…) paired with 24 hand-crafted colour palettes. All free to preview.
